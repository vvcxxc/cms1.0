Event Log Sheet 事件记录表

当一个工序的控制图中出现以下一个或多个现象时，将被视为超出控制。
A process is considered to be out-of-control when any one or more of the following symptoms occur.
 

  • 1) Any individual data outside of the specification limits. 任何超出规格限以外的单值
  • 2) Any point outside of the control limits. 任何超出控制限以外的点
  • 3) 7 points in a row on same side of centreline. 连续7点位于中心线的同一侧
  • 4) 6 points in a row, all increasing or all decreasing. 连续6点上升或下降
  • 5) 14 points in a row, alternating up and down. 连续14点交替上下变化
